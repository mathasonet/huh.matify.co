i dream of tears, i dream of floating
falling slow with nothing showing
in the dark i am glowing
a weightless thing that’s merely floating.

i saw the sun, the lion’s singing
the witch escaped: her words unstinging.
a restless heart, in time, is beating
it calls to join that hopeful singing.

there is no time, the voice will lament
the lion feasts until it is spent.
out goes the sun, a final event:
in this silence there is no lament.
