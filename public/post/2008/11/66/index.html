It Couldn't Be Her (Prologue)

Nov 10, 2008 · 1 minute read
Uncategorized

It was all too
   vivid;
a life she’d never
   touched.

  It burned
itself in her retinas.
  It lingered
in all her senses.
  It wasn’t
    hers.

    Who
  was she
in this fourth dimension?
    How
  did she
become this person?
    Could
this have been her
  all along?

    No!
  she cried,
    these
  aren’t mine
to hold on to.
There is a life
  I must
    relate;
  else it
will be all for
    naught.

This boy
    will
    live.

This was written as the prologue to an unfinished story. The first scrap of prose writing attached to it will be posted momentarily.

I’m still deciding if I want to take it somewhere.
