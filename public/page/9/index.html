April 1

Apr 2, 2011 · 1 minute read
Uncategorized

I led the wind
   Oh he’d left no trace
back to the time
   spent up every
where we grew up
   ward of my mind’s
eyes pained with guilt
   free moments.

Consent

Mar 14, 2011 · 1 minute read
Uncategorized

I twist the experiences between my toes
until they fray and sever
'fore I drop them to the valley below
where no seeker, at least, not one I know
would not be lost forever.

A home they make this far below
free from neglect or age.
Were they to stay connected still
immediately would I have lost the will
and ceased from it all, I’d wage.

Instead solace is to be found in dreams:
dawning hope across my darkened brow.
To dwell on these without much thought
to whatever pain inaction wrought,
is a reality life will not endow.

Mar 14, 2011 · 1 minute read
Uncategorized

Courage knows little of the hollows of my heart,
knows little of supporting dreams.
At the threshold of change
amongst piling claims
of inaction,
it hides.

With it, so have I.

What remains.

Dec 9, 2010 · 1 minute read
Uncategorized

Of all I have left
that survived the frost,
I cling to my island.
It holds me,
weathered,
as the pressure slips
and I lose my grip.
Fleeting,
fleeting is the warmth
from my fingertips
and toes;
I watch it escape me.
Desparately reaching out
to reclaim what’s mine,
what remains.
