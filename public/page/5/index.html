Apr 10, 2014 · 1 minute read
Uncategorized

at least his arms, lord.
i have no internal compass
to direct myself in their absence.

or even his hands, father?
with their sinewed grip
on this flighty heart.

dayspring of a hope misplaced,
protector of a man disgraced,

i’ve lost his comfort, abba,
and for my wickedness and sin:
i’m left to know the dark again.
